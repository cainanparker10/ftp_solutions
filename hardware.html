TracBox


Real-time GPS Locations

The TracBox allows tracking of all the assets on your site in real-time. TracBox sends periodic updates to the Integrated Management System (IMS), where you can view all of your assets and their status, no matter where they are.


Sensor Data

Interface your TracBox with a multitude of hardware types. Sense and display readings such as:

  • Flow Rates*
  • Pressure*
  • Voltage
  • Temperature*
  • Level*
  • Current*



    • *TracBox has an 8-channel ADC capable of measuring voltages up to 60vDC. Any sensors will need to have their outputs scaled to this voltage to be read without any extra hardware/sensing circuitry. TracBox can also read 4-20mA outputs, as well as pulse per second outputs. There are currently up to 7 available input/output pins that can be configured to suit almost any sensing application or hardware.
